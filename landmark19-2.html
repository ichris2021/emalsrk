TO: EMAL MAN
FROM: JAMES
SUBJECT: THE MIRROR

There was an EMAL MAN who heard the words and was given the thought patterns and actions of a CLUTCH PLAYER MAN. He looked into the mirror and thought he was one. However, when he turned away, he immediately forgot what he looked like and what an EMAL MAN was. He then blended in to the faceless crowds who stand for little and fall for a lot.

Another EMAL MAN heard the same words and was given the same thought patterns and actions of the CLUTCH PLAYER MAN. He looked in the mirror and saw he was one. When he turned away he remembered, believed, and acted like an EMAL MAN. He applied the key two minute DRILL to himself each day. He knew what he was and what he looked like. He was now able to recognize himself and other EMAL MEN.

This EMAL MAN received the GIFT FROM GOD
PEACE, POWER, and JOY
that passes all understanding.
HE NOW CAN EXPERIENCE --
TOTAL RELEASE IN THE ARENA.

Love,
Your friend, JAMES

Prov. 27:17
