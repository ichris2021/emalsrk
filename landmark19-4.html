SUCCESS ROAD 2001

You walk away from the mirror asking yourself some genuine thought-provoking questions. "Who and I? What do I stand for? Who and what do I believe?" Like the four quarters of a football game, each of these questions is very important, in fact they are the most important questions to answer in your life.

As you walk out of the emply locker room, you see on the sideline ahead of you an EMPTY WHEELBARROW. How did it get there? It wasn't there before when you went in the locker room. Then you remember the story of the promoter, the high wire man and the wheelbarrow. Do you have the belief and courage to JUMP IN IT AND GO FOR IT? Or do you stand by the side and watch? You are the only one who can answer that.
