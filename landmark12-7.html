  • The Clutch Player has POSITIVE SELF-EXPECTANCY knowing that life is a self-fulfilling prophecy -- THEY EXPECT GOOD THINGS TO HAPPEN - and they often do.

  • A Clutch Player is a TOUGH MINDED OPTIMIST knowinf that belief, persistence, and perseverance are essential in accomplishing dreams, desires, and goal sets.

  • A Clutch Player displays HIGH LEVEL CONSISTENCY and COMMUNICATES foten with an UPBEAT STYLE. They GENUINELY CARE about those whom they lead.

  • A Clutch Player is one who KEEPS HOPE ALIVE!!
