DO YOU GET IT? YOU GOT IT - GOOD!

Pick up some of those blue rocks. You now drive on with new feelings of vim, vigor and vitality and a new awareness of what this adventure is all about. Exciting changes are starting to take place within yourself.

You continue up the mountain pass and come to another scenic view
where you find Landmark 5. Get the SRK bag, sit on the observation chair - relax - Lock on and Learn. (The Potential - Performance Gap). This SRK is in the form of another key illustration, poem and plaque. It gives you a vivd picture of where
you are (the I Am Zone) and where you can go (the I Can Be Zone). It also shares with you the acronym on how to get there. Be sure to find the keys to excellence in this outstanding illustration.

When you finish at this landmark, pick up some black rocks and drive on.
