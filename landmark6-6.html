The Desire - Goal Setting Game

We need to start to build desires in these areas and then GOAL SET to reach them. We should focus on one or two at a time making sure they are all compatible with each other. Some are short while others are long range, and some are a reflection of needs while others are wants -- both of these are important. As we begin to form the habit of doing this a new confidence and enthusiasm will emerge.

In our EMAL Football Program we have 4 key categories to build our desires and Goal Sets on.
    1 - BLUE CAR ATTITUDE
    2 - SKILLS AND SYSTEMS
    3 - FITNESS
    4 - TEAM BUILDING

Read over the Basic Desire-Goal Set list for each, add any others of your own and then

HIT YOUR HOT BUTTON AND START TO GO FOR EM!
You revise and renew these each week. During the season you turn these in and get feedback from your coaches to further motivate you. You also have a teammate to help you be accountable. During the summer try to get someone to feedback to you and keep you accountable. You dad, mom, brother, friend, teammate.
