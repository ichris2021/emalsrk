Clutch Plays for Clutch Players

There are approximately 160 plays in a college football game. However, in any close game there are only three or four plays that really make the difference in the outcome of the game. You never really know when these palys are coming -- that's why every play is so key. You must play in the NOW to be ready to take advantage of these plays when they come.

The following game situations are when these plays do occur a high percent of the the time. These determine which side Big Mo will be on and can have a direct effect in the outcome of the game.

3rd down long > 4 yards or more

3rd down short > Less than 4 yards

4th down short > Less than 2 yards

Red Zone + Green Zone

Last 2 miutes of 1st half

Last 2 minutes of the game

Overtime

Unusual
