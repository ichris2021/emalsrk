SUCCESS ROAD 2001

DID YOU GET IT? GOT IT! GOOD!!
You go to the stadium parking lot, get back in your Blue Car and drive on. As you are reflecting back on the extraordinary experiences you have had on this trip on the SUCCESS ROAD, you haven't really paid much attention to where you were going and now unexpectedly you find yourself -- where? That's right! You are back in teh park by the lake where your trip began. You shake your head - this is really hard to believe.

You get out of your car and take that ol' 7th inning stretch, then as you look at the lake you see - wait a minute. Wait a minute - you see another SRK Landmark 20.

You walk over to teh lakeside and from among the rocks you reach under the Landmark. There is no black and gold bag there but there is a roll of parchment scrolls with SRK written on the outside of them. You sit down on the rocks, slide the leather tie off them, unroll and Lock on and Learn. (Parable of the Rocks)
